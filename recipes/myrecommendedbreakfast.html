My Recommended Breakfast

Description

My breakfast is eggs and pancakes with the sides of hash brown, weenie, and sausage

Ingrediants

  • Egg
  • Pancake batter
  • Cooking spray
  • Sausage
  • Weenie
  • Hash brown
  • Bacon
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix pancake batter with water until you feel there is enougfh to make two pancakes.
  2. Heat up pan for around 1 to 2 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Place half of pancake batter in pan until you see holes surrounding pancake then flip it. Do the same with other half
  4. Place the rest of the ingrediants except for the egg and cook them.
  5. Place egg and once everything else is ready.
