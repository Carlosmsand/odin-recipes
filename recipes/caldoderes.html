Caldo de Res

Description

Also known as Mexican Beef Soup this is a broth that has many vegtables besides just the beef and can also be added with rice. The duration to cook this meal depends if you use a pressure cooker or not but the estimated time is three hours. The serving size for this recipe is 8.

Ingrediants

  • 2 pounds beef shank, with bone
  • 1 tablespoon of vegtable oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons ground blackpepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 carrots
  • 1/4 cup of cilantro
  • 1 potato
  • 2 ears corn
  • 2 chayotes
  • 2 limes
  • 1 medium head cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sliced pickled jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4 radishes

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into half inch pieces
  2. Heat heavy soup pot until very hot. Add the oil at the bottom. Add the meat and bones.
  3. Add onions and let cook for 1 hour on low heat.
  4. Pour in water and add vegtables
  5. Ladle soup into large bowls.
